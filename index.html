Introduction

Study Related Activity

The one and only Orientation Committee has strived to bring you, in close association with the always marvelous Parentsday Committee (PC), your first ever working group/tutorial/course/thingy/whatever on university! Since, like, ever! The tutorial will involve you grinding your gears on Deductive Reasoning, Linear Algebra and your first lines of code in Java! Buckle up for your first taste of what we hope will at all not similar to your first stressfueled and self-hatred inducing Functional Programming tutorial. Enjoy!

The Parents day Committee (PC) organises the annual Parents Day. On this day, the parents of AI students can get to know our study programme, the university, and CognAC. This way they get a peak in the daily lives of their kid(s). Which usually happens somewhere in April, so keep posted!

As stated before, this activity serves to provide you a first peek into working groups, tutorials and the more active part of courses you will encounter during your AI Bachelor. We hope this will give you a better understanding of what you can expect on a day to day basis and teach you a thing or two. This part of the day will take approximately 1 hour. We advise you to spend around 20 minutes on the Artificial Intelligence related assignments, like logic and math. This way you have plenty of time left to enjoy the fun programming assignment.
